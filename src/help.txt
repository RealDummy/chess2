Commands to enter:
    perft <depth>
        -calculate the total moves for depth many turns. 
        Depths larger than 4 may take a while.
    fen <fen>
        -tranform position into entered fen.
    fen show
        -see fen for this position
    show
        -see board
    exit
    reset
    help

anything else is interpreted as a uci move
